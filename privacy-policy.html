Privacy Policy

Panic to Plan

Last updated: March 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Panic to Plan handles information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

The current version of Panic to Plan is designed to work locally on your device.

  • We do not create user accounts.
  • We do not collect personal information on our servers.
  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking tools.

Information You Enter

The app may store information that you type into it, such as tasks, notes, and planning items, so that the app can continue to function when you return.

How Data Is Stored

Information entered into Panic to Plan is stored locally on your device. In the current version of the app, that information is not synced to a remote server by us.

Data Sharing

We do not share your information with third parties in the current version of the app.

Data Retention and Deletion

Data entered into the app remains on your device unless you remove it from within the app, clear the app’s data, or uninstall the app.

Children’s Privacy

Panic to Plan is not designed to knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If features change in the future, including cloud sync, account creation, analytics, or advertising, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those changes take effect.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: privacy@vexelapps.com
